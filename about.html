About us

Crime Cases Worldwide is a non-profit organisation that aims to educate people around the world on unsolved and interesting crime cases by providing the most up to date information on the cases listed. The standards we uphold are high in regards to providing access to the most intersting cases you have never heard before, we promise you that the cases you will find on our website will be engaging, eye-catching and invigorating to read as you venture deeper and deeper into the world of true crime cases.
